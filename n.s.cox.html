About Me

Hi, I'm Neil. And if you've never before met a vinyl-spinning, Elizabethan verse-spouting, sea
anemone poking writer, actor, DJ & world traveler, I'd like to think you're in for a treat. I play
heroes and underdogs - especially heroic underdogs. And if I can go to sleep knowing I made
you dance and Shazam my vinyl music (I only spin vinyl) it's been a good day.
